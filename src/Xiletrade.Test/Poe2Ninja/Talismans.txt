Rarity: Currency
Owl Talisman
--------
Stack Size: 2/10
--------
Sceptres: Allies in your Presence have 8% increased Cast Speed
Foci: 10% increased Cooldown Recovery Rate
--------
Place into an empty Rune Socket in a Focus or a Sceptre to apply its effect to that item. Once socketed it cannot be retrieved but can be replaced by other socketable items.
